

*No* theorem is "proof"!

Theorems are explanations *of* facts - and it's *facts* that prove or disprove theorems.

Darwinian Theory doesn't prove evolution to be true - the facts in the real world (such as examples of fossils and stratigraphy and DNA etc) prove the Theory to be true.

Ultimately the proof/disproof of the efficiency/inefficiency of Capitalism/Communism must come from the comparison and evaluation of historical before and after facts e.g. East and West Germany, North and South Korea, Iron Rice Bowl China versus Free Market China. 
The facts definitively disprove SVT!
